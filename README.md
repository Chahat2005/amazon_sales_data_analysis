## 📈 Key Insights
🛍 Product Popularity

A few categories dominate the dataset with the highest number of products, showing strong focus on high-demand product segments.

⭐ Customer Engagement

Customer reviews are concentrated in a small number of categories, indicating strong demand in specific segments.

💰 Pricing Strategy

Most products fall in the low to mid-price range, showing that Amazon mainly targets budget and mid-range customers.

😊 Customer Satisfaction

Most products have ratings between 4.0 and 4.5, indicating generally positive customer feedback.

🏷 Discount Strategy

Some categories offer significantly higher discounts, showing strong competition in those markets.

📉 Discount vs Rating

There is a weak relationship between discount and ratings, meaning customers value product quality more than discounts.
